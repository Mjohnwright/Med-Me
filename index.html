About Med ME

Dosage Maintenance and Administration

At MedME we manage your PRESCRIPTIONS. Enter the information from your pharmacy's prescription label and we will create a text alert schedule notfying you it is time to take your meds. Simply enter your medication name, your dosage amount and the time that you are to take the medicine. We'll also inform you of any known drug interactions and side effects between your prescriptions. Great for those who care for the elderly.
